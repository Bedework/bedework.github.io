The Venerable Bede, from whom Bedework takes its name, was a highly influential monk and scholar from the area of Northumbria in Britain. In 725AD he wrote the treatise, "On the Reckoning of Time".

Pronunciation:

Bede is pronounced "bead". Bedework, likewise, is pronounced "beadwork".
